Public Shopify Application.

There are two types of public apps, listed and unlisted. You can install listed apps from the Shopify App Store.
Unlisted apps don't appear in Shopify App Store search results or categories. Instead, you can install them from the app's App Store listing or the developer's website. Both listed and unlisted apps must pass the Shopify App review process.

Custom Shopify Application.

Custom apps can be used to add features to your Shopify admin or to extend your online store to other platforms using custom storefronts. Custom apps are built exclusively for your Shopify store and aren't listed on the Shopify App Store.
Shifty solutions make a custom app for you without needing API access to your store and access to your Shopify admin. You can install a custom app by clicking an install link provided to you by us.

Private Shopify Application.

Private apps are available only to your store and aren't listed on the Shopify App Store. You can create a private app for your store in the Shopify admin.
