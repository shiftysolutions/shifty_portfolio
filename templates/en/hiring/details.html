Project Manager - Ecommerce

Tangier / Full Time / 2 Open Positions
A few points on culture
  • We are not an agency and never will be. We are an extension of our clients’ teams. 
  • We don’t have hierarchies. 
  • We value freedom and independence. 
  • We should enjoy working together.
  • We don’t like or tolerate BS.
We are looking for two project managers with e-commerce experience to join our growing team.
What You 'll Do
  • Work with clients to define, detail and strategize the scope of work
  • Schedule weekly meetings with clients and manage the relationship with their teams
  • Document the process, scope, tasks and timelines through Basecamp 
  • Take full ownership of of the project and deliver on schedule
  • Work with our technical partners and platforms to solve issues or challenges
  • Provide ecommerce consulting on improving ecommerce KPI and the ecommerce tech stack
  • Design basic wireframes to clarify to the client and internal teams the UX strategies and directions
  • Handle hands-on store management requirements: setups, configurations, data entry, app installs, etc. 
  • Perform quality assurance before the project is delivered to clients
  • Work with our technical and creative teams to identify and produce adequate and efficient solutions 
  • Support client with instructions and documentation for self-management of store and third parties 
Our Ideal Candidate
  • Solid e-commerce experience : Understands all aspects of e-commerce very well, including how disciplines are aligned with each other.
  • Strong experience with Shopify : Has an intimate understanding of the platform, its technology and its ecosystem of apps. 
  • Good user experience design : Knows and stays updated on user experience trends, conversion rate and e-commerce optimization strategies.
  • Problem solver &detail oriented : Identifies red flags and connects the dots quickly to keep projects on track.
  • Emotionally intelligent : Good listener, self-aware, receptive to feedback and has the ability to develop a relationship with different people
  • Agency project management experience : 3+ years
